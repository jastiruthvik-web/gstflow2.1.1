Three Broken Workflows

Where the hours go.

01
📱

WhatsApp at Midnight

The scene: It's 11:43 PM. A client sends you a blurry photo of a crumpled invoice taken under their car dashboard light. "Bhai please add this." Tomorrow is the filing deadline.

This happens dozens of times per month. Every client. Different formats, different quality, different levels of urgency. There's no structure, no portal, no system. Just your WhatsApp, exploding.

~4 hrs/month per client chasing uploads Zero audit trail 100% manual sorting
02
⌨️

Manual Data Entry into Tally

Once you have the invoice — if you can read it — every single field needs to be typed by hand into Tally. GSTIN. Invoice number. Date. Taxable amount. CGST. SGST. HSN code. Supplier name. Total.

That's 9 fields. Per invoice. For a client with 50 invoices per month, that's 450 manual keystrokes of data — every month, without fail, with zero margin for error.

200 invoices = 2 full working days Repetitive. Permanent. Scaleless.
03
⚠️

Errors & GST Penalties

One wrong digit in a GSTIN. One invoice missed because it came in a 200-message WhatsApp thread. One transposition error in a taxable amount. These aren't hypothetical — they happen constantly.

And the cost? A GST penalty notice. Client trust, gone. Hours spent rectifying. Sometimes ₹10,000–₹50,000 in penalties. For a typo. On an invoice you typed by hand at 10 PM.

1 mistake = more than a year of software cost No confidence scores = no safety net
