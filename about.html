Our Story

Built by people who
watched CAs suffer.

GSTflow started with a simple question: why is the most important professional in India's MSME compliance chain also the most underserved?

⚡

Why We Built GSTflow

We watched Chartered Accountants — brilliant professionals who navigate the complexity of India's tax code with ease — lose their weekends to a problem that had nothing to do with their expertise.

Manually typing invoice numbers into Tally. Chasing clients on WhatsApp at midnight. Fixing errors from crumpled invoice photos. Month after month. Permanently.

The problem isn't incompetence. The problem is that nobody built them the right tool. ClearTax built for large firms. Tally built for accounting. Nobody built for the pre-filing gap that consumes 15–20 hours of every solo CA's month.

GSTflow is our answer to that. Not a pivot or a growth hack — a direct response to a real problem we've seen up close. We're building it in Hyderabad, talking to CAs every week, and obsessing over making it exactly right.

"We're not trying to disrupt GST software. We're trying to give 72,000 solo CAs their weekends back." — We at GSTflow
What We Stand For

Three principles we
won't compromise on.

01

Built for the CA, not the enterprise

Every design decision starts with the solo practitioner. Not the Big Four. Not the 50-person firm. The CA who manages 40 clients alone and files GST for all of them every month.

02

Accuracy over speed

We'd rather flag a field for review than silently pass a wrong value. Confidence scores are transparent. The CA always knows exactly what AI is certain about and what it isn't.

03

Simple enough to actually use

If onboarding a client takes more than 5 minutes, we've failed. If the dashboard needs a tutorial, we've failed. GSTflow must work the way a CA's practice actually works.
