GSTR-3B Hard-Locked
After Filing

CAs can no longer edit pre-filled GSTR-3B returns after filing. Accuracy before filing is now mandatory — not optional.

This is a regulatory forcing function. Every CA in India must now ensure their input data is perfectly clean before filing begins. There's no editing it afterward. No undoing a mistake. No correcting a GSTIN after submission.

This single policy change turns GSTflow from a "nice to have" into an essential professional tool for every CA who files GST returns. The urgency is not manufactured — it's written into law.

⚡

Every CA in India now needs a pre-filing verification layer. GSTflow is that layer.
